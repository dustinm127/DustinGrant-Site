Dustin Grant is an international award-winning freelance filmmaker based in Dallas, Texas, and the owner of Pink Mouse Blue Mouse Productions.

He grew up on a small tree farm in rural Louisiana, which shaped his love of landscape and his curiosity about people. That background still shows up in his work. He is drawn equally to open spaces and cities, to quiet observation and human complexity.

After spending two decades in the corporate technology world, Dustin left to focus on creative work full-time. His approach favors clarity, restraint, and purpose. He believes the best work is simple, intentional, and honest.

Over the past decade, his work and curiosity have taken him around the world, from Iceland and Central America to U.S. national parks. He works across film, writing, and photography, and continues to explore new forms of storytelling.

At the core of everything he does is a desire to understand how things work, why people connect to stories, and how art can say more by doing less.
